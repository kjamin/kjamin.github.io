What can students get out it?

  • Opportunity to work on meaningful projects that make a difference in your community.
  • Real consulting experience and skills that can be applied in future internships and jobs.
  • A tight-knit community of equally-motivated and -driven students from diverse backgrounds.
  • Access to exclusive consulting recruiting resources such as firm events, interview prep workshops, written guides, alumni network, etc.
  • Ability to step into multiple leadership roles throughout club progression.

Where do our members go after college?

Our alumni go on to work at top consulting firms, investment banks, and tech companies across the United States. Alumni often stay engaged with our organization through mentorship and networking opportunities. This is fostered through our culture of paying it forward.
