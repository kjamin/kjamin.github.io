The world’s largest student consultancy.

180 Degrees Consulting is the world’s largest consultancy for non-profits and social enterprises. With over 10,000 carefully trained volunteer consultants and over 200+ branches, we provide strategy services to further social good amongst organizations around the world.


Our branch at the University of North Carolina at Chapel Hill selects ~30 students to work on semester-long projects with clients from a variety of industries. Consultants come from diverse backgrounds and academic interests to provide creative solutions to your organization’s problems.


Social impact is our purpose, and we work to help create change.

Welcome – A Letter from our Presidents.

Welcome to 180 Degrees Consulting at UNC! We are the UNC-Chapel Hill branch of the largest international student-led consultancy group and the premier student consulting group on campus. Whether you are a client interested in our services or a student interested in joining our organization, we thank you for your interest in 180!

Our consulting group works with nonprofits, socially-minded businesses, and other organizations across the world in navigating their strategic barriers and achieving their values and goals. We started 3 years ago working with smaller startups within the Triangle area and have rapidly expanded to work with national and international organizations such as Lenovo, Meals on Wheels, and Ronald McDonald House, just to name a few of our clients. We carefully select our consultants to ensure that we have a diverse, driven team of the best and brightest. With consultants from over 10 majors with various professional aspirations, our greatest offering is our diversity of perspectives within our shared passion for experiential learning and creating impact. In our past projects, we have worked with our clients on a multitude of services such as growth strategy, data science, financial analysis, investment research, leadership restructuring, and consolidation.
